History

Ferrari Rafting Company was founded in 1990 by Nestor Ferrari. By 1994 the company found its permanent home in Mendoza, where Nestor and his wife Stella turned a fledgling rafting company into what is now one of the most respected outfitting companies Nestor was president of the company for 29 years until his retire in July of 2019. Today, Nicolas and Lucas, Nestor and Stella’s sons, are leading the company. They’re dedicated to furthering the company’s commitment to the preservation of wild places and creating meaningful outdoor experiences for our guests values that have been at the core of Ferrrari company for thirty three years.
